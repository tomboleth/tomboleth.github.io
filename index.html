FAQ

  • What's the rule ?

    Pretty simple :  players bet , one win .

  • What's my Tomboleth balance ?

    Smart contracts recommended method of sending funds after an effect is using the withdrawal pattern. That's why your balance stays on the contract until you withdraw.

  • Can I play with my balance ?

    Yes. You don't need to send ETH again if you have a positive balance.

  • Can I play w/o a Web3 browser ?

    Yep. See here.

  • Show me the code !

    You can see the contract on Etherscan and all our code is on GitHub
